\textbf{Problem 1.} (IMC 2010, Day 1, Problem 1)
Let \( 0 < a < b \). Prove that
\[
\int_a^b (x^2 + 1) e^{-x^2}\, dx \;\geq\; e^{-a^2} - e^{-b^2}.
\]
\\

\textbf{Problem 2.} (IMC 2011, Day 2, Problem 3)
Determine the value of
\[
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \ln\!\left(1 + \tfrac{1}{n}\right) \cdot \ln\!\left(1 + \tfrac{1}{2n}\right) \cdot \ln\!\left(1 + \tfrac{1}{2n+1}\right).
\]
\\

\textbf{Problem 3.} (IMC 2014, Day 1, Problem 1)
Determine all pairs \( (a, b) \) of real numbers for which there exists a unique symmetric \( 2 \times 2 \) matrix \( M \) with real entries satisfying \( \operatorname{trace}(M) = a \) and \( \det(M) = b \).
\\

\textbf{Problem 4.} (IMC 2010, Day 1, Problem 3)
Define the sequence \( x_1, x_2, \ldots \) inductively by \( x_1 = \sqrt{5} \) and \( x_{n+1} = x_n^2 - 2 \) for each \( n \geq 1 \). Compute
\[
\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{x_1 \cdot x_2 \cdot x_3 \cdots x_n}{x_{n+1}}.
\]
\\

\textbf{Problem 5.} (IMC 2011, Day 1, Problem 2)
Does there exist a real \( 3 \times 3 \) matrix \( A \) such that \( \operatorname{tr}(A) = 0 \) and \( A^2 + A^t = I \)?
(Here \( A^t \) is the transpose of \( A \) and \( I \) is the identity matrix.)
\\

\textbf{Problem 6.} (IMC 2010, Day 2, Problem 3)
Denote by \( S_n \) the group of permutations of \( (1, 2, \ldots, n) \). Suppose that \( G \) is a subgroup of \( S_n \) such that for every \( \pi \in G \setminus \{e\} \) there exists a unique \( k \in \{1, 2, \ldots, n\} \) with \( \pi(k) = k \). Show that this \( k \) is the same for all \( \pi \in G \setminus \{e\} \).
\\

\textbf{Problem 7.} (IMC 2014, Day 2, Problem 3)
Let \( f(x) = \dfrac{\sin x}{x} \) for \( x > 0 \), and let \( n \) be a positive integer. Prove that
\[
\left| f^{(n)}(x) \right| < \frac{1}{n+1},
\]
where \( f^{(n)} \) denotes the \( n \)-th derivative of \( f \).
\\

\textbf{Problem 8.} (IMC 2011, Day 1, Problem 4)
Let \( A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_n \) be finite, nonempty sets. Define the function
\[
f(t) = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \sum_{1 \leq i_1 < i_2 < \ldots < i_k \leq n} (-1)^{k-1}\, t^{|A_{i_1} \cup A_{i_2} \cup \ldots \cup A_{i_k}|}.
\]
Prove that \( f \) is nondecreasing on \( [0, 1] \).
\\

\textbf{Problem 9.} (IMC 2010, Day 1, Problem 5)
Suppose that \( a, b, c \) are real numbers in the interval \( [-1, 1] \) such that
\[
1 + 2abc \;\geq\; a^2 + b^2 + c^2.
\]
Prove that for all positive integers \( n \),
\[
1 + 2(abc)^n \;\geq\; a^{2n} + b^{2n} + c^{2n}.
\]
\\

\textbf{Problem 10.} (IMC 2016, Day 2, Problem 10)
Let \( A \) be an \( n \times n \) complex matrix whose eigenvalues all have absolute value at most \( 1 \). Prove that
\[
\| A^n \| \;\leq\; \frac{n}{\ln 2}\, \| A \|^{n-1}.
\]
Here \( \| B \| = \sup_{\|x\| \leq 1} \| Bx \| \) and \( \| x \| = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n |x_i|^2} \).
