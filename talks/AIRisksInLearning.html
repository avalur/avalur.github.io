Unstoppable Cheating
Current publicly available LLMs are capable of solving any university assignment for a few minutes. In a year any laptop and mobile phone can be used. How to check students' understanding and conduct online exams?
Perhaps an even more difficult question is how to conduct online selection of schoolchildren for university?
Who earned your rating on Codeforce, who solved Kaggle for you, what do you basically understand and can do without AI?
Most often, people learn from other people, a human teacher is needed to act as a role model for the student. A carbon robot is not made of flesh and blood, so it cannot be a role model for the student.
Another important point is socialization, and definitely we need other people to socialize with.
One of the main properties of a person is the ability to adapt very quickly, both to changing external conditions and to the mood and level of students. Most likely, this can be implemented in AI systems, but not yet.
Despite the performance benefits then using AI, people who collaborated with GenAI on one task and then transitioned to a different, unaided task consistently reported a decline in intrinsic motivation and an increase in boredom.
When AI handles the most challenging parts of work - like the critical thinking or tailored feedback needed for performance reviews - it removes what makes tasks engaging. People feel disconnected from their output and lose their sense of control over the work.
