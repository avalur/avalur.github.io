Let's go on to a few observations about modern education from our teachers.

They NOTED the incredible speed of learning today. Essentially, as soon as new knowledge emerges, it becomes almost immediately available to everyone. This speed makes a significant impact.
For instance, IMAGINE we discover something on Twitter today. By tomorrow, we've already added a new skill to our repertoire. In just a week, all of our students have acquired this skill.
That's why the CRUCIAL skill nowadays becomes the ability to solve unsolved problems.
ALSO our teachers highlighted certain essential activities for the development of critical thinking.
Those activities include crafting mathematical PROOFS and checking them,
DEBUGGING problems in code or systems,
tasks related to system ENGINEERING, including robotics,
and, importantly, you have to engage in HUMAN discussions and debates.
Each activity, in its own way, sharpens our ability to evaluate information critically and make reasoned judgments.
