Course annotation

The aim of the course is to demystify AI.

  • Starting with Python, Pandas and fundamental machine learning techniques
  • Exploring statistical learning models, linear and logistic regression, decision trees, and ensemble methods
  • Studying deep learning, covering multi-layer neural networks, stochastic gradient descent, backpropagation, optimization techniques, and more
  • A little bit more advanced topics such as recurrent, convolutional neural networks, and general pre-trained transformers (GPT) will also be addressed

Flipped format with live discussions of course materials, interesting challenges, and testing your understanding of machine learning

What does the flipped course format mean in our case?

You will study theory and solve basic practical tasks through video recordings and presentations before face-to-face classes.

In class, we will discuss the material covered with each student individually or in small groups to find and eliminate all possible problems and misunderstandings together.

This format has several advantages over traditional lectures:

  • Each of you will go through the material at your own pace
  • The course includes a large number of auto-checked tasks and theory quizzes that allow you to get instant feedback and test your knowledge
  • You can study at any time and continue exactly from where you left off
  • In homework blocks, you will need to complete and submit at least one item out of three, and you can choose any topic that interests you
  • There are four assignment blocks in total per semester, with three weeks to complete each one
